A free community repair session where volunteer experts work side-by-side with visitors to fix broken household items — clothing, lamps, small electronics, clocks, vacuums, AV gear, and more. Bring your broken thing, learn to fix it. Coffee and fruit served. RSVP by emailing a photo and description of your item. 11am–2pm.

{{< event

Learn crochet without a hook — your hands are the tool. This beginner-friendly 2-hour class at NYC Resistor covers the basic chain, stitches, and joining, culminating in a foundational piece you can work into a scarf, headband, or small bag. Taught by NYC artist Lo, who describes it as a meditative practice. Mask required. 18+. 5–7pm.
