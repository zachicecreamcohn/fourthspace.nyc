Welcome to the first ever issue of Fourth Space! Issues are posted Thursday afternoons and contain events for that Friday through the follow Thursday. I hope you enjoy! And If you have any recommendations or want to submit an event for consideration, feel free to reach out to me at fourthspace.nyc@zachwcohn.com

{{< event
  title="The Cemeteries of New Orleans"
  price="$12"
  org="Brooklyn Brainery"
  trains="2 3 B Q"
  url="https://brooklynbrainery.com/courses/the-cemeteries-of-new-orleans"

Art historian and Green-Wood Cemetery tour guide JR Pepper lectures on New Orleans' unique "Cities of the Dead" — their European and Caribbean architectural heritage, ornate tombs, and the preservation challenges facing these remarkable burial sites. Pepper is an adjunct at Brooklyn College and author of *Buried Boston*; he's currently writing a book on New Orleans cemeteries.
